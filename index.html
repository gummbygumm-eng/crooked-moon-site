Last Session Notes

The Cemetery Gate

The party entered the cemetery searching for lanterns that must be lit to contain a gate. Something waits on the other side, and nobody wants it getting out.

  • Goal: light the lanterns.
  • Danger: the gate is holding something back.
  • Mood: graveyard fog, low fiddle, whispering dark.
